## About Slay the Spire 2

Slay the Spire 2 is a roguelike deckbuilding strategy game that expands the mechanics and world introduced in the original Slay the Spire. Players build unique card decks, encounter challenging enemies, and climb a mysterious tower filled with unpredictable events.

Each run presents different cards, relics, and enemies, making every playthrough unique. Strategic decisions about deck composition, combat tactics, and resource management determine whether players can reach the top of the Spire.
